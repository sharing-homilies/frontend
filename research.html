Conference: Faith & Media 2026

Mapping Homily Themes Across Irish Parishes

Kieran O. • Maeve S. • Patrick N.

Abstract. We analyze five years of homilies to surface dominant theological themes and regional variations. Our pipeline blends audio transcription with structured semantic uplift to create a searchable thematic index.

Keywords. Semantic uplift, Ireland, themes.

Published February 2026

Conference: Digital Liturgy 2025

Audio-to-Insight: Building a Homily Knowledge Graph

Aoife R. • Liam C. • Grace T.

Abstract. We present a knowledge graph derived from parish audio that links scripture references, emotional tone, and pastoral intent. The graph supports thematic browsing and research queries.

Keywords. Knowledge graph, audio, metadata.

Published November 2025

Conference: Church Futures 2026

Temporal Focus in Contemporary Homilies

Siobhan L. • Dermot F.

Abstract. Using temporal focus annotations, we quantify how sermons balance past scripture, present community needs, and future hope. Findings highlight seasonal shifts across dioceses.

Keywords. Temporal focus, seasonality, liturgical year.

Published January 2026

Conference: Pastoral Analytics 2025

Audience Focus and Pastoral Care Signals

Niamh K. • Owen M. • Tara B.

Abstract. We identify audience focus patterns across homilies, highlighting how sermons address families, youth, and community needs. The study links audience focus to emotional tone and action items.

Keywords. Audience focus, pastoral care, tone.

Published October 2025
